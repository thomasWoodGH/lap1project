Tiber Typhoon

RULES

  • • Divide the group into teams
  • • Each team will take turns choosing a Roman numeral.
  • • When the number is clicked a Popup will prompt with the question.
  • • Answering the question correctly will score points!
  • • The team with the most points at the end wins!
  • -- POWER-UPS --
  • • Decimate (Soldiers) - Choose a team to half the score of.
  • • Nero's Fiddle - Halves your own points!
  • • Veni, Vidi, Vici - Set everyone else's score to 0.
